Discover Indonesian Culture

From Sumatra to Papua, explore the diverse cultural heritage of the Indonesian archipelago

Aceh - Ulee Balang

Traditional royal costume from Aceh, symbolizing nobility and high social status

Sumatra - Pepadun

Elegant white and gold ceremonial dress from Lampung, representing purity and nobility

Jawa - Beskap

Formal Javanese men's attire from Solo and Yogyakarta, rich in philosophical meaning

Kalimantan - Sapei Sapaq

Traditional Dayak ceremonial attire from East Kalimantan, worn for important rituals

Sulawesi Selatan - Baju Bodo

Elegant Bugis-Makassar women's dress with unique color symbolism and gold accessories

Papua - Koteka

Traditional attire from Papua's highlands, made from natural materials with deep cultural significance

About Nusa-Dwipa

Nusa-Dwipa is an innovative multiplayer educational game designed to introduce players to the rich cultural heritage of the Indonesian archipelago. Through immersive technology, players can explore traditional houses, collect authentic costumes, and learn about the diverse cultures that make up Indonesia.

Our mission is to preserve and promote Indonesian cultural heritage through interactive digital experiences, making learning about culture engaging, accessible, and fun for people of all ages.
